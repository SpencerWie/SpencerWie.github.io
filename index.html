About Me


I started doing development work in the early 2000s back in elementary school making and games with ActionScript and GameMaker along with art and running, back then I knew I wanted to be a software developer. To me software development is an artform where you can create whatever you want but instead of a brush you use logic, it's something you can interact with instead of just something to look at.

In highschool I focused more on web and application development, with a focus on full stack development. I also worked on personal projects and answered questions on Yahoo Answers and later StackOverflow. Then started working as a web developer with IUPUI a bit before I started my freshman year in college; in my later college years I worked as a TA and a software developer for DataXStream. Here I grew an interest with AI with a focus on machine learning and took every AI course both grad and undergraduate.
